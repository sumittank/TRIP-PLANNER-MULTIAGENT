About AI Travel Planner

AI Travel Planner is a production-quality multi-agent travel planning platform built with LangGraph, FastAPI, and Groq (LLaMA 3.3). A supervisor agent routes your request to specialist agents that run in parallel, a critic reviews the plan, and a formatter delivers structured itineraries with checklists.
